设 S,TS,TS,T 都是可重序列

maxS=∑S⊊TminT(−1)∣T∣−1maxS=\sum_{S\subsetneq T}minT(-1)^{|T|-1}maxS=∑S⊊T​minT(−1)∣T∣−1

minS=∑S⊊TmaxT(−1)∣T∣=1minS=\sum_{S\subsetneq T}maxT(-1)^{|T|=1}minS=∑S⊊T​maxT(−1)∣T∣=1
