The only spell with which he can damage them is a fire ball. If Polycarp hits the $ i $ -th archer with his fire ball (they are numbered from left to right), the archer loses $ a $ health points. At the same time the spell damages the archers adjacent to the $ i $ -th (if any) вҖ” they lose $ b $ ( $ 1<=b<a<=10 $ ) health points each.

As the extreme archers (i.e. archers numbered 1 and $ n $ ) are very far, the fire ball cannot reach them. Polycarp can hit any other archer with his fire ball.

The amount of health points for each archer is known. An archer will be killed when this amount is less than 0. What is the minimum amount of spells Polycarp can use to kill all the enemies?
